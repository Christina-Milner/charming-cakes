Meet the Baker

Hi, I'm Jane, and I've enjoyed baking ever since I cut out biscuits with my mother as a child.

After many years of delighting people with birthday cakes and pastry gifts, I decided to take my passion to the next level and pursue certificates in Cake Art and Sugar Flowers.

I now run a small business serving the Townsville area. Charming Cakes has provided many satisfied customers with delicious cupcakes for corporate events, modern wedding cakes, whimsical birthday cakes and much more.
